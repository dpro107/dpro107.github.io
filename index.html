About Sierra Model United Nations

"As the young leaders of tomorrow you have the passion, energy and commitment to make a difference. What I would like to really urge you do is to have a global vision. Go beyond your countries; go beyond your national boundaries." -Ban Ki-moon

Welcome to the crazy and diplomatic world of Model UN! This will be a journey that will teach you about the world, let you develop life changing skills and make friends with excellent people you'd never met otherwise. You will learn to research, speak publicly, think critically and develop leadership skills. You will travel, have fun, laugh, cry, learn the art of teamwork and come out inspired and empowered to make the world a better place.
LNMIIT MUN 2k19 brings to you this efficacious opportunity where you can explore yourself and this world. The novice will get a taste of diplomacy and the extremes will get a perfect platform to keep their points on, getting a chance to debate amongst the consummate delegates.
LNMIIT MUN consists of UNHRC, DISEC and Lok Sabha. You can try out your skills in the one you prefer to, because ,"your preference is our priority". Also, it will be a two day conference scheduled on 12th Jan and 13th Jan 2k19.
So, get yourself registered in this much awaited version of LNMIIT MUN and you are assured to name it as "worth waiting", once you be a part of it. Mark the dates!

Organiser's Desk

Dear friends, It gives me immense pleasure to bring you the 5th edition of LNMIIT Model United Nations, to be held on 12th-13th of January, 2019 at LNMIIT, Jaipur. It is my privilege to lead the team which will be your host for this conference. Every MUN that I have been a part of, has strengthened the belief, that an MUN is the most complete form of debate. It is a great opportunity to enhance skills in research, writing, public speaking, problem solving, consensus building and crisis management. It gives you the honor of being an ambassador of a country. More importantly, it entrusts you with the task of solving global issues. With LNMIIT MUN, we intend to provide you a platform to discuss and deliberate on realistic issues through an assemblage of committees, led by a brilliant Executive Board. The team is committed to make your experience enjoyable and rewarding through a structured environment. We hope to make the conference a landmark MUN and aspire for your support in doing so. I welcome you again and look forward to see you all here at LNMIIT MUN 2019.

- Ayushi Agarwal

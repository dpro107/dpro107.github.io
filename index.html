ABOUT BIKANER MUN


Amidst the undulating sand dunes of the desert city of Bikaner, we bring forth the Bikaner MUN edition. The beatific city of Bikaner is no less appealing but with a MUN to top it all off is similar to icing on the cake. Even though considered and left of as an asinine and ludicrous endeavor by many, quiet contrary to expectations the first edition of the Bikaner MUN defied all expectations and carved a niche for itself in the MUN circuit. In this place, diplomacy comes forth in its pure and truly colourful form. It is the meeting of ideas and clashing of opinions and emotions and the cocktail formed is worth the wait. The cacophony of voices, the underlying urgency to leave a mark, the struggle of a neophyte and the waltz of delegate bearing the mark of experience all come into view and make for a truly beautiful sight. As conceived by our convener, Mr. Devesh Agarwal, this MUN is all about Good Ideas and True Innovations. We welcome you all in this little abode of ours live and make some of the best memories of your life.

FROM THE SECRETARY-GENERAL



Honorable prospective delegates,

Esteemed Advisers,

Dear friends,

It is my immense pleasure and honor to welcome you to the first edition of ITM Model United Nations Conference, organized by and hosted by . To my elation, I will be your courteous host this July, from 16 th to 18 th . The conference will be held in the royal city of Gwalior. Even though this is the first edition of the conference, but the team working behind the scenes is highly experienced and one of the best. The conference will see both national and international delegates from various nations. Our expectations from this conference are very high and I assure you that you won’t be disappointed! I would gladly invite you to participate as a delegate in this conference. Be prepared for some most exciting experience and debate! The motto of ITM Model United Nations is and we are striving hard to uphold the virtue of this motto. ITM MUN will see six committees, which will empower you to think and see the world from a very different perspective. All the committees envisage the nation’s and the world’s exponential issues. I would also like to take this opportunity to mention that the executive board of these committees are very resourceful and are probably some of the most experienced. We are working hard to ensure that this MUN leaves a strong and lasting impression in the MUN circuits and we hope to set new benchmarks via this conference. We hope to change the world for better with this MUN conference and ensure that in this sea of time, we leave a legacy which can be exemplified, which will not be buried under the dust.

In conclusion, I would like to remind you all that everyone can point out the mistakes in this not so perfect world but few have the courage to amend those mistakes to make this world a better place, if not for oneself, then for the future. The only constant in this world is change and time for change is here yet again.

What is to say more? Apply now!

Parth Pareek

Secretary General ITM MUN 2017

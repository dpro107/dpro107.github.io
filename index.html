ABOUT ITM MUN


A Model United Nations is an academic simulation of United Nations. It is a way of experiencing and coming as close to clockwork of UN as you can in the capacity of student, unless your father is a diplomat! Have you ever wondered how the work is done in the corridors of power and how those diplomats address the pressing concerns of the world while keeping the interests of their nation in mind, if that is the case, then ladies and gentlemen, MUN is the thing for you. There are countless number of MUNs taking place across the globe all over and ITM MUN is one such MUN and it is going to set a standard for rest of the MUNs which will be hard to meet any time soon. This is going to be the first edition of the ITM MUN but the Organising Committee is no pushover. With one of the finest Secretary General, the first edition is not going to climb up the ladder but will already be there!The first edition of the MUN is going to be held on 16-17 th July 2017 in the city of Gwalior. The host is going to be The ITM University, Gwalior. There is a huge misconception that MUNs do not help you anyway in professional or personal life but many who held this belief now stand corrected. MUNs help you achieve a finesse and delicacy and at the same time make you more audacious. They help you tell other peoples lies and bluff with a straight face and most importantly MUNs teach you to create a grey from a black and a white on which everyone can tread upon. These are soft skills which make a successful professional and a happy individual, and at ITM MUN, these will be available in abundance. We welcome you all to ITM MUN 2017.

FROM THE SECRETARY-GENERAL



Honorable prospective delegates,

Esteemed Advisers,

Dear friends,

It is my immense pleasure and honor to welcome you to the first edition of ITM Model United Nations Conference, organized by and hosted by . To my elation, I will be your courteous host this July, from 16 th to 18 th . The conference will be held in the royal city of Gwalior. Even though this is the first edition of the conference, but the team working behind the scenes is highly experienced and one of the best. The conference will see both national and international delegates from various nations. Our expectations from this conference are very high and I assure you that you won’t be disappointed! I would gladly invite you to participate as a delegate in this conference. Be prepared for some most exciting experience and debate! The motto of ITM Model United Nations is and we are striving hard to uphold the virtue of this motto. ITM MUN will see six committees, which will empower you to think and see the world from a very different perspective. All the committees envisage the nation’s and the world’s issues of exponentially rising importance. I would also like to take this opportunity to mention that the executive board of these committees are very resourceful and are probably some of the most experienced. We are working hard to ensure that this MUN leaves a strong and lasting impression in the MUN circuits and we hope to set new benchmarks via this conference. We hope to change the world for better with this MUN conference and ensure that in this sea of time, we leave a legacy which can be exemplified, which will not be buried under the dust.

In conclusion, I would like to remind you all that everyone can point out the mistakes in this not so perfect world but few have the courage to amend those mistakes to make this world a better place, if not for oneself, then for the future. The only constant in this world is change and time for change is here yet again.
